About

While I was in high school I created an app called MCAS Prep, this is the second version that I completed after I graduated during Make School Summer Academy. MCAS Prep allows students in Massachusetts to study for the Massachusetts Comprehensive Assessment System(MCAS). This app easily allows students to take many test for free and automatically get their score.

I made this app for all those students in the state of Massachusetts that are required to take the MCAS of any subject. I hope this app helps all of those students out there to easily be able to study and succeed in high school.

This app was created during the summer of 2015 in MakeSchool Summer Academy. For more information visit https://www.makeschool.com
