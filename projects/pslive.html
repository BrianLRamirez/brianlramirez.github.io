PlayStation Live: A fun hackathon project.

PlayStation Live is an Apple TV application that allows users to watch all their friend’s live streams in one place! Although this product is not available to PlayStation users (and it was never meant to be), it displays the capabilities of what I was able to do in two days of non-stop coding.

This app won Second Place at the PlayStation summer hackathon! Sadly I do not own the copyright to the code I wrote for this hackathon, so I cannot link to the application or link to the code. However you can take my word for it, it was an awesome project.
