Hibi: Study Japanese vocabulary everyday

日々 (Hibi) which means daily in English, is an easy way to learn new Japanese vocabulary.

I created this app while trying to learn new Japanese vocabulary for class. It’s not meant to be fancy or anything like that, it is simply a way to see pictures of objects and see the Japanese translation as well as the Kanji, Hiragana and or Katakana.

This app was inspired by the Mainichi chrome extension.
